Appointments are short. Your experience isn't.

Most migraine sufferers arrive at appointments with incomplete notes, scattered apps, or a foggy memory of the last few weeks. Providers, meanwhile, need clear patterns — not pages of raw logs.

Sentrya bridges that gap by summarizing your experience into focused, relevant insights your provider can actually use.

How Sharing Works

Designed for Real Appointments

1. Track Naturally

Log symptoms, triggers, and daily context in plain language — by voice or text — without rigid checklists.

2. Patterns emerge quietly

Sentrya highlights trends over time, such as frequency, severity, sleep impact, weather correlations, and treatment response

3. Choose what to share

Before an appointment, select exactly what to include — date ranges, summaries, or specific trends. Nothing is shared automatically.

4. Share clearly, not excessively

Generate a concise, readable report your provider can review quickly — during or before your visit.

What Providers Actually See

Clarity, Not Data Overload

Providers don’t need every log entry. They need:

1. frequency and severity trends

2. changes over time

3. response to treatments

4. clear timelines

Sentrya reports are structured to support medical decision-making — without forcing you to explain everything from scratch.
