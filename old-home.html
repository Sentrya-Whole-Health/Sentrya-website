FAQ

Your migraine questions, answered

Get clear, practical answers about tracking, privacy, sharing, and using your migraine data.

How does tracking work?

Log migraine episodes, symptoms, and related factors. The app organizes your data to help you and your provider identify patterns and triggers.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your information is stored securely and only shared with your provider if you choose. You control what is shared.

Can I share reports?

You can generate reports to share with your healthcare provider, making it easier to inform your treatment.

Which devices are supported?

The app works on most current smartphones and tablets. Check the app store for specific compatibility details.

Is there a free plan?

Yes! We offer a free plan that includes essential tracking features. Upgrading unlocks advanced insights and more sharing options.

How do I get support?

Support is available through in-app help, email, and our online knowledge base. We respond within one business day.
