Answers for migraine management

Find clear, concise answers to common questions about tracking migraines, sharing data, and using our app to support your health journey.

How does migraine tracking work?

Our app lets you log migraine episodes, symptoms, and triggers in just a few taps. Over time, you can spot patterns and share relevant data with your provider.

What information can I share?

You control what you share. Export summaries or detailed logs to your clinician, focusing only on what’s helpful for your care.

Is my health data private?

Yes. Your data is stored securely and never shared without your consent. You decide what to keep private and what to share.

Do I need a subscription?

The free plan covers basic tracking. Upgrade to Insights+ for advanced insights and easier sharing. Choose the plan that fits your needs.

Can I use the app with my provider?

Yes. Share your migraine history and patterns directly with your healthcare provider to support more informed conversations.

What devices are supported?

Our app works on most modern smartphones and tablets, as well as desktop. You just need an internet connection.
