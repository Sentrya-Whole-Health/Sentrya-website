What This Beta Is

The Sentrya beta is a small, private testing phase focused on learning what actually makes migraine tracking easier in real life.
‍
This is not a polished, final product. Some features may be unfinished, change over time, or behave unexpectedly.
‍
That’s intentional — the goal of this beta is to observe real usage, identify friction, and improve clarity before a wider release.

You don’t need to use Sentrya perfectly or consistently.
‍
Using it in the way that feels natural to you — even if that means stopping for a while — is part of what helps us learn.

What to expect during the beta:

During the Sentrya beta, you’ll have access to the core tracking experience while we focus on learning what works well and what quietly gets in the way.
‍
You may notice:
Features that evolve or change over time
Occasional bugs or rough edges
New tools appearing as testing progresses
‍
This is a normal part of the process. The beta is designed to be flexible, and nothing requires you to use Sentrya in a specific way or on a specific schedule. Your experience — including moments of confusion, frustration, or even disuse — helps inform what Sentrya becomes.

What We Ask of Testers

We ask very little of our beta testers. There’s no required schedule, no daily check-ins, and no expectation that you use Sentrya perfectly or consistently.

The most helpful thing you can do is use the app in a way that feels natural to you. If something feels confusing, frustrating, or unexpectedly helpful, we appreciate hearing about it. Short feedback, screenshots, or simple notes are more than enough — and sharing feedback is always optional.

If you stop using Sentrya at any point, that information is just as valuable. Understanding when and why the app doesn’t fit into daily life helps us improve it.

What Testers Receive

As a thank-you for participating in the Sentrya beta, approved testers will receive lifetime full access to Sentrya once it launches publicly.

This includes access to all core features as they evolve over time, without a subscription fee. Lifetime access is tied to your individual account and is our way of acknowledging the time, trust, and insight early testers contribute.

There’s no obligation to provide feedback or meet usage goals. Your participation — in whatever form it takes — is appreciated.

Lifetime access refers to access for the lifetime of the Sentrya product and includes core features available to standard users.
